Welcome to WaitStaff Calculator

Hi! Welcome to WaitStaff Calculator!
This app, is created to help you serve your customers in a faster and more dynamic way, as never seen before!
To start, enter the details of each Customer's Meal in the New Meal Section. Enter the base meal price in USD, the Tax Rate of your location (eg. 8) and finally the Tip Percentage (eg. 10).
Just that easy! In seconds you can come back to your customers with their final Charges, leaving the thedious maths to us, while you focus on Making Money!!
The Best part, is that by the end of the day, you can simply go back to your app, and click on the My Earnings section where you will find How Much Money You Made Today!, how many meals you served, and your average tip per meal!
Enjoy!
